ABOUT

Bianca Pasello is a Brazilian-American designer creating bold garments by redefining classic punk elements into practical, gender neutral designs.

All garments are handcrafted and range from original one of a kind designs to up-cycled pieces. Everyone has the right to feel comfortable and confident in exploring their personal style, and as a woman who has always felt more comfortable in more masculine styles of clothing, she has a very personal insight into gender neutral style. There is such beauty in the unconventional and Bianca Pasello seeks to amplify off-kilter styles and inspire confidence through her work. Each garment is intended to give the woman embrace of acceptance. Bianca Pasello is an all encompassing look that gives the wearer a sense of lifestyle rather than just a garment.
